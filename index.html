About Vayurvya

Magnificent things are very simple.

Welcome to Vayurvya, a celebration of a decade of excellence by Aero Club Nitte! In honor of our 10th-year anniversary, we proudly present a national-level competition featuring thrilling RC modeling contests, enlightening talks, engaging paper presentations, and a host of other exciting events. Join us as we soar to new heights and embrace the spirit of innovation in the world of aeronautics. Let's make Vayurvya a testament to our passion for aviation and the remarkable journey of Aero Club Nitte.
